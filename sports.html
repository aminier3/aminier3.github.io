Sports

I have done many sports since I was little like, basketball, soccer, softball, dance, and track. Over the years I have really started to like basketball. I have done many travel leagues throughout the years and played at my old school, Hamilton Elementary. Starting in the fall I played for the Lane Tech girls basketball team on JV. We played very good this season and got to the semifinals for playoffs. I also got to play in a super cool tournament over the summer. We got to go against good teams and ended up winning the championship! After my team won we got to play during halftime in the bulls game. I really enjoy playing sports and it is a big part of my life.
